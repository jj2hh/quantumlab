CROWN & BRIDGE

Precision-engineered fixed prosthetics for ultimate esthetics and durability.

Diagnostic Wax-up

Clinical Case Visualization

The essential baseline for smile makeovers and comprehensive full-mouth rehabilitations. We accurately map out the ideal tooth anatomy, occlusion, and function on diagnostic models, providing doctors and patients with a clear, predictable preview of the final treatment outcome.

IPS e.max®

Lithium Disilicate Glass-Ceramic

Renowned for its unmatched optical properties, IPS e.max delivers pristine, lifelike esthetics that mimic natural tooth structure flawlessly. Perfect for anterior crowns, veneers, and inlays, this lithium disilicate material offers superior shade matching and outstanding biocompatibility for premium cosmetic results.

Full Metal / Gold

Full Cast Restorations

Engineered for ultimate longevity and biocompatibility. Our full cast gold and noble alloy restorations provide exceptionally precise marginal fit and minimal wear against opposing dentition. Recommended for second molars and heavy bruxism patients where clearance is limited.

PFM

Porcelain Fused to Metal

A time-tested classic in restorative dentistry. Our PFM restorations combine the structural reliability of a customized metal substructure (precious or non-precious alloy) with the natural beauty of hand-stacked feldspathic porcelain. Excellent for long-span bridges and high-stress load scenarios.

Zirconia

Full Zirconia / Layered Zirconia

Our premium Zirconia restorations offer the perfect balance of exceptional strength and natural esthetics. Full Zirconia is ideal for posterior teeth requiring maximum durability against high occlusal forces. Layered Zirconia combines a strong zirconia core with hand-layered porcelain, delivering lifelike translucency for highly demanding anterior cases.
